Welcome to Maya's personal blog

My name is Maya

I would be using this media as my personal web page to share my journey with you. It starts here. I have never been much of a writer and the idea of writing a blog was making me a little nervous. But I have set a challenge to myself that I should do it because who knows, I could put smile on someone's face just by sharing a story of my journey.

I'm originally from Indonesia and moved to Wellington, New Zealand in 2004. I have been living in this beautiful little capital ever since. As a Victoria University graduate in Marketing and Information Systems, I've grown my interest in Web Design and Internet Marketing and decided to pursue a career within this industry.

I spend a lot of time on the Internet, reading about current trends, news, and google is my number 1 friend!

When I'm not working, you'll find me exercising at the gym. My current fitness focus are on the core strength and body endurance. I do boxing twice to three times a week and sometime I like to mix my exercise combination with running and weights. I have a strong interest in the martial art type of sports, Muay Thai or Thai kickboxing.
I also like watching a bit of footy/rugby league on the weekend.
